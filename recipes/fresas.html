Fresas con Crema

Ingredient List

  • Fresh Strawberries: 1 lb
  • Sour Cream: 2 cups
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk: 1 cup
  • Evaporated Milk: ½ cup
  • Vanilla Extract: ½ teaspoon

Steps

  1. Remove and discard the stems from the strawberries. Slice the fruit into thin slices and transfer to a medium bowl.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, add sour cream, sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk and vanilla extract. Whisk together to combine.
  3. Scoop ½ cup sliced strawberries into 6 bowls or cups. Then top each cup with ½ cup sweet cream mixture.
  4. Serve immediately with a spoon or store in the fridge for up to 5 days.
